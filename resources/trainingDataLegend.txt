How to write technical marks:

[FROM][TO][SPEED][SHAPE]

FROM & TO:
    A-X single uppercase letter (17 options)
SPEED:
    single uppercase letter from options:
        X - extended walk
        W - walk
        V - collected walk
        I - extended trot
        T - trot
        Y - collected trot
        G - extended canter
        C - canter
        O - collected canter
        H - halt
SHAPE:
    single uppercase letter from options:
        L - line
        C - circle clockwise
        F - circle anti-clockwise
        B - big circle clockwise
        G - big circle anti-clockwise
        S - small circle clockwise
        T - small circle anti-clockwise
        D - semi-circle clockwise
        P - semi-circle anti-clockwise
        A - arc 1/4th of circle clockwise
        z - arc 1/4th of circle anti-clockwise
        H - halt
